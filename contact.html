Contact Us

Email Address

Please direct all inquiries to: ghibliportal@gmail.com

What to Email

We welcome your questions and feedback! Please email us about:

  • Account issues (e.g., login problems, payment queries)
  • Refund requests (include username and payment details)
  • Custom Ghibli artwork requests (contact our artist at 8103356828 too!)
  • General feedback or suggestions

How to Email

Follow these steps for a quick response:

  1. Use your registered email address or mention your username.
  2. Subject line: Clearly state your purpose (e.g., "Refund Request - [Username]").
  3. Keep your message concise and include relevant details (e.g., date of payment).
  4. Send to ghibliportal@gmail.com. We aim to reply within 48 hours.

User Dos and Don'ts

To ensure smooth communication, please follow these guidelines:

  • Do: Be polite and specific in your email.
  • Do: Check your spam folder for our reply if delayed.
  • Don't: Send multiple emails about the same issue—wait for a response.
  • Don't: Include sensitive info like passwords; we’ll never ask for them.
